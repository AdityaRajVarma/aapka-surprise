05 — Dil Se

Tumse ek baat kehni thi —

Tum jo ho, woh bahut khaas hai.
Na isliye ki main bol raha hoon —
balki isliye ki maine dekha hai.

Tumhara dard, tumhari muskaan,
tumhara gussa, tumhara pyaar —
sab kuch real hai. Aur main
sab kuch sun'na chahta hoon.

Jab bhi bura lage — yaad rakhna,
ek banda hai jo genuinely
tumhara khayal rakhta hai.

Bas itna hi. Koi drama nahi. 🌸

— Main yahin hoon, hamesha 💕
